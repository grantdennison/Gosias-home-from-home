MY PRIORITY IS ALWAYS PROTECTION AND SAFETY!

I encourage good behaviour, independence, confidence and teach children good manners, taking turns, sharing and respecting each other. Each child in my care is able to participate in a range of stimulating and educational activities appropriate for their age and stage of development. These activities include arts and crafts, baking, messy play, physical play such as music and movement. I take the children to childminder’s play groups, baby bounce where they have fun with singing rhymes. I also do outings to the park, zoo, farm, library, forest, as well as enjoying time playing in my home from home setting.
